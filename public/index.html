Who are we?

Our Services

At Vinea Care we understand that every individual is different and has specific needs, hence we appoint each of our clients to our experienced coordinators to ensure the care plan you receive will meet all your needs. We encourage dialogue with our clients and enjoy your reviews on our services. Our dedicated team have a 24/7 line to ensure you receive the support you need. Get in touch with us to find out more.

Live-in care

A fully trained carer will be living with you and will support you with your daily needs, keeping you comfortable and independent from the comfort of your own home.
They will assist you with;
- Daily Personal Care
- Specialist/Medical Care
- Handling your dietary and nutritional needs
- Companionship and
- Daily domestic tasks and housekeeping

Palliative Care

Our dedicated caregivers will offer home-based care for individuals with terminal illnesses. We understand that this is a stressful and sensitive time and we hope that our specialist care services and trustworthy support can make a difference. We support our clients, their families, friends, district nurses, and palliative teams through proper Palliative Caregiver selection and a 24/7 access support line.

Respite Care

Have you been caring for a loved one and are now looking for a temporary helping hand? Vinea care has a team of dedicated caregivers who can give you the helping hand you need.

Visiting Care

Let us arrange regular visits where our caregivers can handle tasks such as laundry, house cleaning, meal preparation, and running errands.
